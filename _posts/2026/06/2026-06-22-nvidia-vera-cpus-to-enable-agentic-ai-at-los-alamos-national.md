NVIDIA Vera CPUs will be deployed in new supercomputers at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) to accelerate scientific discovery. This initiative, in collaboration with HPE, aims to unlock agentic AI technologies specifically for scientific applications. The HPE Cray Supercomputing GX5000 architecture will support these advancements, enhancing LANL's capabilities in computational research. This follows NVIDIA's ongoing efforts to integrate AI technologies into supercomputing environments, as seen in their recent partnerships and infrastructure developments. The introduction of Vera CPUs signifies a shift towards more autonomous AI systems in scientific research, potentially transforming methodologies in the field.
