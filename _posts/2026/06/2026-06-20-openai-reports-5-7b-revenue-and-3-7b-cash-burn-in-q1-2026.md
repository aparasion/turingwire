OpenAI's revenue for Q1 2026 reached $5.7 billion, marking a year-over-year tripling, while the company burned through $3.7 billion in the same period. This significant cash burn includes over $2.3 billion allocated for stock-based compensation. OpenAI's current reserves stand at $73 billion, indicating a robust financial position despite the high expenses. This financial performance underscores the ongoing investment in AI development and infrastructure, which may influence competitive dynamics in the AI sector, particularly with companies like Anthropic also vying for market share. The figures reflect a trend in the industry where rapid growth is often accompanied by substantial operational costs, as previously noted in related coverage by [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/openai-tripled-revenue-to-5-7-billion-in-q1-but-burned-through-3-7-billion-to-get-there/).
