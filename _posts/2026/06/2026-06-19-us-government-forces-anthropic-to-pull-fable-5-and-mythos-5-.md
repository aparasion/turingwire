The US government mandated Anthropic to withdraw two AI models, Fable 5 and Mythos 5, last week due to national security concerns. Cybersecurity researchers have labeled the government's action as 'dangerous,' suggesting it may inadvertently enhance Anthropic's brand visibility. Anthropic has countered that similar security vulnerabilities, referred to as jailbreaks, exist in other AI models, indicating a broader issue within the industry. This incident follows previous scrutiny of Anthropic's models and highlights ongoing tensions between AI development and regulatory oversight. Practitioners should note that the removal of these models could affect the availability of competitive alternatives in the market.
