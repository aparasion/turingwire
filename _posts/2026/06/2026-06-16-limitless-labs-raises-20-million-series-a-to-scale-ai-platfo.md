The new capital will enable Limitless Labs to enhance its technology, which focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence to optimize manufacturing workflows. This platform aims to reduce waste, improve product quality, and increase operational efficiency—key factors that are becoming increasingly important in a competitive market. The precision manufacturing sector is projected to expand significantly, with a growing emphasis on automation and data-driven decision-making.
