NVIDIA emphasized that the critical question for advertisers is not whether to adopt AI, but whether their infrastructure can support it at the required speed and scale. This statement was made during the Cannes Lions festival, where NVIDIA showcased its latest AI technologies aimed at reshaping the advertising and marketing sectors. The company is responding to increasing demands for robust AI solutions that can handle large-scale operations efficiently. This follows NVIDIA's ongoing efforts to integrate AI into various industries, including its recent initiatives in AI infrastructure in France. Practitioners in advertising and marketing must now consider upgrading their AI infrastructure to meet these emerging demands, as the landscape shifts towards more sophisticated AI applications.
