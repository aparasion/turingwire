PP-OCRv6, an Optical Character Recognition model, has been released on Hugging Face with an updated size of 34.5 million parameters, up from the initial 1.5 million. This model supports OCR in 50 languages, significantly expanding its usability for multilingual applications. The increase in parameters suggests enhanced capabilities, likely improving accuracy and performance in text recognition tasks. This follows previous advancements in OCR technology discussed in related stories on fine-tuning techniques and model benchmarking. For developers, the expanded language support means they can now deploy OCR solutions that cater to a broader audience without needing separate models for each language. More details are available on the [Hugging Face Blog](https://huggingface.co/blog/PaddlePaddle/pp-ocrv6).
