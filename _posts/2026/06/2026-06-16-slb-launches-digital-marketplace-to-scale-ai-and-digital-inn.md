By providing a centralized platform for digital tools and services, SLB aims to empower energy companies to leverage advanced technologies more effectively.

The digital marketplace is expected to serve as a hub for various stakeholders in the energy sector, including operators, service providers, and technology developers. This initiative aligns with SLB's broader strategy to integrate digital innovation into its offerings, which has become increasingly crucial as the energy industry navigates a transition towards more sustainable practices. The marketplace will facilitate access to a range of AI-driven solutions, potentially transforming how companies approach operational challenges and decision-making processes.
