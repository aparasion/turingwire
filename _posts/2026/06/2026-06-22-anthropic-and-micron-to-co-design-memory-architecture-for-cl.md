Micron is set to co-design AI memory architecture with Anthropic as part of a multi-year deal to supply memory for Claude's infrastructure. Tom Brown, co-founder of Anthropic, emphasized that "Memory is critical to training and running Claude." This collaboration follows Micron's significant investment in Anthropic's Series H funding round, contributing to a stock surge of over tenfold for Micron in the past year. Critics have raised concerns that such circular deals may be inflating a bubble in the AI sector. This partnership may enhance the efficiency and performance of Claude, impacting how developers utilize memory resources in AI applications.
