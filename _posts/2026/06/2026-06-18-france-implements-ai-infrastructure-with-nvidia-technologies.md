META: France is advancing its AI initiatives with NVIDIA technologies, enabling startups to deploy applications.

France is launching AI infrastructure in collaboration with NVIDIA, following a year of planning initiated at the NVIDIA GTC Paris during VivaTech. This initiative aims to enhance local AI capabilities, with reports indicating that "AI agents are running in production" and startups are actively deploying applications. The collaboration marks a significant step in France's commitment to becoming a leader in AI within Europe. This follows previous coverage of NVIDIA's involvement in AI advancements, including their partnerships for global cloud buildouts and research on self-training robots. Practitioners can expect increased access to robust AI tools and frameworks as these infrastructures come online, facilitating faster deployment of AI solutions.
