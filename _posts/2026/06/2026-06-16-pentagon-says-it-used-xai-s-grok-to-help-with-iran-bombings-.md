Grok, an AI tool developed by xAI, is designed to enhance decision-making processes by analyzing vast amounts of data. The Pentagon's admission of using Grok in real-world military operations signals a shift in how defense agencies leverage AI technologies. This move aligns with broader trends in the military sector, where AI is increasingly seen as a critical asset for operational efficiency and strategic planning. The use of such advanced technology raises questions about the ethical implications and accountability in military actions.
