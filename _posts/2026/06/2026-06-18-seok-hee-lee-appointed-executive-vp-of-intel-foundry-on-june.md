On June 18, 2026, Seok-Hee Lee was appointed as the Executive Vice President of Intel Foundry. In this role, he will oversee all advanced packaging, system integration, back-end technology development, and back-end manufacturing, according to Intel Corporation. This leadership change aims to accelerate Intel's development and manufacturing capabilities in the foundry space, which is critical as the company seeks to strengthen its position in the semiconductor market. This follows Intel's recent efforts to enhance its foundry services, as highlighted in previous announcements regarding partnerships and innovations. For practitioners, this appointment signals a potential shift in Intel's operational focus, which may impact future collaborations and technology offerings.
