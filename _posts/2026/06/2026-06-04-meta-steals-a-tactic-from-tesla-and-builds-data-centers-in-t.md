By implementing this innovative solution, Meta aims to significantly cut down its data center expenses, which have become a pressing concern in the current economic climate.

However, Meta's potential adoption of this strategy highlights the growing trend among tech giants to seek more flexible and cost-effective solutions amidst rising expenses.
