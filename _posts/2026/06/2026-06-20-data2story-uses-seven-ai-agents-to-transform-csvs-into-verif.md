Data2Story utilizes a Data Journalist Agent developed by Oxford and Stanford to convert CSV files into interactive news articles. The system achieves a 93 percent rate of verifiable source links in its output, significantly enhancing the credibility of generated content. Additionally, 74 percent of readers preferred the AI-generated articles over those written by humans, indicating a shift in audience reception towards AI-assisted journalism. This development follows a trend in AI applications within media, as seen in recent advancements like OpenAI's ChatGPT enhancements. The integration of multiple AI agents in this process may influence how practitioners approach automated content generation and verification in journalism.
