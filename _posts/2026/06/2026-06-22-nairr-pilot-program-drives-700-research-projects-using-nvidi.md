The U.S. National Science Foundation's National Artificial Intelligence Research Resource (NAIRR) pilot program has facilitated 700 research projects over the past two years. NVIDIA has contributed its AI infrastructure to this initiative, providing researchers with dedicated access to advanced computational resources. This collaboration enhances the capabilities of scientific research, allowing for more complex and data-intensive projects. The NAIRR pilot program exemplifies the growing integration of AI in scientific discovery, following NVIDIA's recent advancements in AI software for scientific applications. For practitioners, this means greater access to powerful AI tools for research initiatives. More details can be found in the [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/nairr-scientific-research-ai-infrastructure/).
