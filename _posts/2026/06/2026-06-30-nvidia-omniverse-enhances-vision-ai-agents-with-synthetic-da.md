NVIDIA's Omniverse platform now supports enhanced workflows for Vision AI agents, focusing on the use of synthetic data and fine-tuning techniques. This development aims to improve the accuracy of AI agents that convert video data into operational intelligence. The integration with OpenUSD facilitates 3D data interchange, allowing for more efficient model training and deployment. Practitioners can leverage these workflows to create more robust AI applications, particularly in environments requiring real-time data processing. This follows NVIDIA's ongoing efforts to optimize AI infrastructure, as seen in their recent initiatives for AI server cooling and agentic AI capabilities at research facilities like Los Alamos National Laboratory, according to [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/vision-ai-agent-skills-omniverse-metropolis/).
