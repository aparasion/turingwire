NVIDIA has developed a complete GPU-accelerated computing stack over more than a decade, now integrated with Anthropic's newly announced Claude Science, an AI workbench for scientific research. This week, Anthropic unveiled Claude Science, which aims to facilitate advanced AI applications in life sciences. The BioNeMo Agent Toolkit will enable researchers to leverage NVIDIA's infrastructure for accelerated AI model training and deployment, significantly enhancing their computational capabilities. This integration is expected to streamline workflows for life sciences researchers, allowing for more efficient experimentation and data analysis. For practitioners, this means access to advanced tools that can handle complex biological data more effectively, potentially accelerating research timelines. More details are available on the [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/claude-science-bionemo-agent-toolkit/).
