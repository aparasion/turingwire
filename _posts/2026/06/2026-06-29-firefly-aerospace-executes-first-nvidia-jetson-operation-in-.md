Firefly Aerospace has conducted the first operation of NVIDIA Jetson in lunar orbit. This marks a significant milestone in the use of NVIDIA's AI technology for space exploration. The successful deployment demonstrates the capability of Jetson to support autonomous operations in challenging environments such as the Moon. This follows previous advancements in AI infrastructure by NVIDIA, including their work with the NAIRR Pilot Program, which has driven numerous research projects leveraging NVIDIA's technology. The integration of Jetson in lunar operations could enhance future missions by enabling real-time processing and decision-making capabilities in space.
