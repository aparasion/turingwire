Austria's State Secretary for Digitalization, Alexander Pröll, has proposed that the European Commission work to attract Anthropic to Europe. This initiative comes in response to the U.S. ban on advanced AI models from companies like OpenAI and Anthropic for foreign users, which has raised concerns about dependency on Chinese AI models. While the proposal aims to bolster AI independence in Europe, it has been described as likely unrealistic. This follows ongoing discussions about AI sovereignty in the EU, highlighting the urgency for local alternatives. The potential relocation of Anthropic could significantly impact the European AI landscape, providing local access to advanced AI technologies.
