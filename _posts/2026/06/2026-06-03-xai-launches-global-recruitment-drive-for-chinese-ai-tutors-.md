The recruitment effort comes at a time when the AI education sector is rapidly evolving, and companies are seeking to leverage diverse talent to enhance their offerings.

The recruitment drive is part of xAI's broader strategy to expand its capabilities and improve its AI tutoring systems. By focusing on Chinese AI tutors, xAI aims to tap into a vast pool of talent that can bring unique insights and expertise to the company.
