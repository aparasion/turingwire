The company is focusing on leveraging AI to streamline processes and improve decision-making. By harnessing data analytics and machine learning, Unilever plans to enhance its supply chain management, optimize marketing strategies, and personalize consumer experiences. This initiative aligns with a growing recognition among major corporations that AI can drive significant operational improvements and foster a more agile business model. Unilever's ambition to scale its AI capabilities is not just about technology; it's also about cultural change within the organization, encouraging teams to embrace data-driven decision-making.
