Anthropic has declared that it no longer requires junior engineers due to advancements in AI, a shift that may signal broader changes across industries. The company warns of an impending economic shock as other sectors adopt similar AI-driven efficiencies. This follows a trend where AI capabilities are increasingly automating tasks traditionally performed by less experienced workers. Anthropic's assertion reflects a growing reliance on AI for operational efficiency, which could reshape workforce dynamics significantly. The implications for hiring practices and workforce composition in tech and beyond are profound, as noted by Anthropic's statement on 'returns on intuition.'
