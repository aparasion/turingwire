Wistron has opened a 324,000 square foot manufacturing facility in Fort Worth, Texas, dedicated to producing NVIDIA AI systems. This marks Wistron's first U.S. manufacturing site, expanding its capabilities in AI infrastructure. The facility aims to support the growing demand for AI technologies, as highlighted by an unnamed source stating, "The AI era runs on AI infrastructure." This development follows NVIDIA's recent announcements regarding the ramp-up of production across various global sites, indicating a strategic push in AI infrastructure manufacturing. The opening of this facility may enhance local supply chains for AI systems, potentially benefiting companies reliant on NVIDIA's technology.
