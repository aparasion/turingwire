# OpenAI's Principles for Government and National Security Partnerships

OpenAI emphasizes three core principles for its partnerships with government and national security entities: responsible AI use, democratic accountability, and public safety. These principles guide how OpenAI intends to collaborate with these sectors, ensuring that AI technologies are deployed ethically and transparently. This approach aligns with OpenAI's broader commitment to fostering trust and safety in AI applications. OpenAI's focus on these principles is critical as it navigates complex relationships with governmental bodies, particularly in light of increasing scrutiny on AI's role in national security. For practitioners, this clarity on OpenAI's guiding principles may influence how they integrate OpenAI's technologies within government-related projects. More details can be found on the [OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/government-national-security-partnerships).
