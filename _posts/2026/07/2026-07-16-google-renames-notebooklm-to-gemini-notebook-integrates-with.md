Google has renamed its tool NotebookLM to Gemini Notebook, enhancing its integration within the Google ecosystem. Each Gemini Notebook features its own cloud computer, catering initially to AI Ultra and Workspace customers. This follows Google's ongoing efforts to deepen the functionality of its AI tools, as seen in previous announcements regarding Gemini models. The integration of the search app with third-party applications allows for expanded capabilities, enabling users to leverage external tools directly within Gemini Notebook. This shift may enhance productivity for developers and teams relying on Google’s AI solutions, as they can now utilize a broader range of applications seamlessly. [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/google-rebrands-notebooklm-as-gemini-notebook-and-opens-its-search-app-to-third-party-integration/) reported.
