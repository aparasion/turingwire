Beijing has mandated the shutdown of humanlike chatbot personas by major AI platforms, including ByteDance and Alibaba, in response to new regulations. This regulatory action specifically targets custom AI companion features, significantly impacting the functionality offered by these platforms. The decision follows a broader trend of increased scrutiny on AI technologies in China, aimed at ensuring compliance with government standards. Practitioners relying on these features for user engagement will need to adapt their strategies accordingly. This development aligns with previous reports on the evolving landscape of AI regulations in China, highlighting the ongoing challenges faced by AI developers in the region.
