ChatGPT has launched a new health feature that enables eligible U.S. users to securely connect their medical records and Apple Health. This integration aims to provide users with personalized insights to better understand their health. The feature leverages data from Apple Health to enhance the user experience and health management capabilities. This follows OpenAI's ongoing efforts to expand the utility of ChatGPT in various sectors, including previous initiatives in journalism and enterprise AI solutions. For practitioners, this development opens avenues for integrating health data into AI applications, potentially improving patient engagement and personalized healthcare solutions.
