China has announced the **World Artificial Intelligence Cooperation Organization**, which will offer **5,000 AI training slots** specifically for countries in the Global South. This initiative was unveiled by **President Xi Jinping** at the **World AI Conference** in Shanghai. The organization is part of a broader strategy to establish a parallel AI governance structure that operates outside of Western influence, as noted by [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/chinas-new-world-artificial-intelligence-cooperation-organization-is-president-xis-clearest-play-yet-for-a-parallel-ai-order/). It aims to strengthen ties with regional alliances such as **ASEAN**, the **African Union**, and **BRICS**. This follows a trend of increasing geopolitical maneuvering in the AI sector, emphasizing the importance of non-Western frameworks in shaping future AI policies.
