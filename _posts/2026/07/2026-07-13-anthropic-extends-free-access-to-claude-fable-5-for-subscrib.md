Anthropic has announced that subscribers will continue to have free access to Claude Fable 5 until July 19, 2026, allowing them to utilize up to 50 percent of their weekly usage limit without incurring charges. This decision comes as a strategic response to pricing pressures from OpenAI's GPT-5.6 Sol and other lower-cost alternatives in the market. The anticipated switch to a pay-per-use model for Claude Fable 5, originally scheduled for today, has not been implemented. This extension may provide developers and businesses using Fable 5 with additional time to evaluate their options in light of evolving pricing strategies in the AI landscape, particularly as competition intensifies. [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/anthropic-extends-free-fable-5-access-for-subscribers-as-openais-gpt-5-6-sol-heats-up-the-pricing-war/) reported.
