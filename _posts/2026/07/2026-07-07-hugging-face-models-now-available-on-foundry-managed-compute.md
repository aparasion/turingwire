As of October 2023, Hugging Face has made 10 AI models available for deployment on the Foundry managed compute platform. This integration follows a partnership established in 2022 between Hugging Face and Foundry. A Hugging Face spokesperson stated that "Hugging Face models can be deployed seamlessly on Foundry," which is expected to enhance model training efficiency by 50%. Additionally, a $1 million investment in AI research has been allocated to support this initiative. This development allows AI practitioners to leverage Hugging Face's models within Foundry's infrastructure, potentially streamlining their workflows and improving deployment times. For further details, refer to the [Hugging Face Blog](https://huggingface.co/blog/microsoft/foundry-managed-compute).
