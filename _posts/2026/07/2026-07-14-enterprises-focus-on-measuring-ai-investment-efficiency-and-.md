Enterprises are encouraged to manage AI investments by focusing on measuring useful work per dollar. This approach aims to improve efficiency and scale high-value workflows, which are critical for maximizing returns in the agentic era. The emphasis on these metrics is essential for organizations looking to optimize their AI strategies. This guidance aligns with broader discussions in the industry regarding the economic impacts of AI, as highlighted by recent warnings from economists and researchers about rapid changes in the job market. For practitioners, this means a shift towards data-driven decision-making in AI investments is becoming increasingly important.
