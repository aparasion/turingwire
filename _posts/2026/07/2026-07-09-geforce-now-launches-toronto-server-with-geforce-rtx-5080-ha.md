GeForce NOW has launched a new server powered by the GeForce RTX 5080 in Toronto. This update, announced during GFN Thursday, aims to provide enhanced gaming performance and more game options for users. The introduction of the RTX 5080 hardware is part of NVIDIA's ongoing efforts to improve cloud gaming experiences. Additionally, the game 'Neverness to Everness' is receiving an update as part of this rollout. This expansion follows NVIDIA's previous announcements regarding specialized AI for workflow integration, indicating a broader strategy to enhance gaming and AI capabilities in tandem. For developers and gamers, this means access to more powerful cloud gaming resources in the Toronto region, potentially improving latency and performance for users in North America. More details can be found on the [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/geforce-now-thursday-toronto-expansion/).
