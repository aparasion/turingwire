Ode, a new joint venture co-founded by Chris Taylor and Eddie Siegel, aims to embed engineers in enterprise firms, challenging traditional consulting models. Backed by Anthropic, Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs, Ode seeks to leverage a handful of engineers to deliver AI services, posing the question: "Can a handful of engineers really do the work of an army of consultants?" This initiative follows Anthropic's recent focus on enterprise solutions, including its Claude Fable 5 model, which has shown strong performance in industry benchmarks. Practitioners can expect a shift in how AI services are delivered, moving from large consulting teams to smaller, specialized engineering teams.
