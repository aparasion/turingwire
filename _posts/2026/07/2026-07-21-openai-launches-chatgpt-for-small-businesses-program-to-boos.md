# OpenAI Launches ChatGPT for Small Businesses Program

OpenAI has launched the **ChatGPT for Small Businesses** program, designed to assist entrepreneurs in building AI skills, automating work, and fostering growth. This initiative aims to empower small business owners by providing them with tools and resources to effectively utilize ChatGPT in their operations. OpenAI states that the program will facilitate the development of AI capabilities among entrepreneurs, enhancing their productivity and operational efficiency. This follows OpenAI's ongoing efforts to integrate AI solutions into various sectors, as seen in their recent initiatives aimed at improving AI deployment metrics and safety standards. For small business practitioners, this program represents a direct opportunity to leverage AI for operational improvements and competitive advantage. More details can be found on the [OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-small-business-program).
