SpaceX has unveiled a slim AI smartphone prototype to investors, which is thinner than an iPhone. The device integrates xAI technology and utilizes a Qualcomm Snapdragon chip. Elon Musk described the smartphone as an 'everything app,' modeled after WeChat, suggesting a comprehensive platform for various services. The smartphone will run on a proprietary operating system, although specific details about its features remain undisclosed. This development aligns with SpaceX's ongoing innovations in AI and technology integration, following recent advancements in their Grok AI tools. For developers, this prototype may signal new opportunities for app development within a unified ecosystem, as indicated by Musk's vision for the device.
