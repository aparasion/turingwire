The model captures the strategic decision-making process of users, who may increase their LLM usage to counteract the effects of detection. The analysis includes empirical observations of word frequencies in arXiv abstracts to validate the model's predictions regarding the detected attribute.\n\n## Results\nThe available text does not report quantitative results.
