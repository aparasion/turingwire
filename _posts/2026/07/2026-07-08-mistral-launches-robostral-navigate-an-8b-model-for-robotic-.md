Mistral has launched Robostral Navigate, an 8 billion parameter model designed for robotic navigation using only a single RGB camera. This model employs the CISPO method for reinforcement learning, enabling robots to navigate unknown environments effectively. Robostral Navigate achieved a performance score of 76.6 percent on the R2R-CE benchmark. This entry into the robotics market follows Mistral's previous focus on proprietary models amid competition with OpenAI and Anthropic, as highlighted by CEO Mensch earlier this year. The introduction of Robostral Navigate may enhance the capabilities of developers in the robotics sector, particularly in environments where traditional navigation systems are inadequate.
