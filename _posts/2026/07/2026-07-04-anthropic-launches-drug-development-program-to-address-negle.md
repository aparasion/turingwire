# Anthropic Launches Drug Development Program to Address Neglected Diseases

Anthropic has initiated a drug development program targeting neglected diseases, aiming to significantly reduce the time and increase the success rates of drug discovery. CEO of Novartis, Vas Narasimhan, stated that AI could cut development time from twelve years to seven or eight years and double the success rate from 8 percent to 16 percent. This move positions Anthropic to directly compete with traditional pharmaceutical companies like Novartis, which often overlook these diseases due to profitability concerns. The launch of this program follows a growing trend in the AI sector to leverage machine learning for healthcare advancements, as seen in previous reports on Anthropic's innovations. Practitioners in the biotech field may find new opportunities for collaboration with AI firms like Anthropic, enhancing their drug development pipelines.
