Meta's new image generation model, Muse Image, features a controversial @-mention capability that allows users to generate images of individuals using their Instagram photos. This functionality has raised significant legal concerns, particularly regarding compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the EU AI Act, as it operates on an opt-out model. The implications for AI developers and companies leveraging similar data practices could be profound, as they may need to reassess their compliance strategies. This follows previous discussions about Meta's AI initiatives, including the slower-than-expected progress on AI agents, as noted by Mark Zuckerberg.
