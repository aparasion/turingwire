Meta's Muse Spark 1.1 API is priced at $4.25 per million output tokens, directly entering the competitive AI API market. This launch follows the release of Grok 4.5 yesterday, intensifying the price competition among AI providers. OpenAI and Anthropic are now under increased pressure to adjust their pricing strategies, as noted by an unverified source claiming "the pressure just got worse." This pricing move positions Meta as a significant player in the AI API landscape, potentially impacting how developers choose between these services. For practitioners, this means a more cost-effective option for integrating AI capabilities into applications.
