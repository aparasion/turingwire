In May, Anthropic achieved a revenue run rate of $47 billion, with projections of $9 billion in revenue for 2025. Menlo Ventures invested $500 million in Anthropic's Series D funding round. Matt Murphy, an investor at Menlo Ventures, noted that this growth is unprecedented in his 25 years of investing, stating he has never witnessed such rapid expansion in previous tech waves, including the internet, mobile, or cloud sectors. This follows Anthropic's recent developments in AI capabilities, which may influence future investment strategies in the AI startup landscape. For AI engineers and product managers, this level of funding and growth signals a robust market confidence in Anthropic's technology and its potential applications.
