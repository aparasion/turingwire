Performance per watt is now identified as the definitive metric for evaluating AI infrastructure efficiency, emphasizing its importance in determining revenue and profitability for AI factories. This metric is directly linked to the number of tokens generated within a fixed power budget, making it a critical factor for AI engineers and technical product managers. NVIDIA asserts that this metric cannot be manipulated and must be achieved through tangible, real-world results. This focus on performance per watt aligns with previous discussions on AI infrastructure efficiency, particularly following NVIDIA's recent advancements in chip technology and AI models, as reported on [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/performance-per-watt-ai-infrastructure-efficiency/).
