S&P Global has downgraded Oracle's credit rating to "BBB-" due to concerns that OpenAI represents a significant financial risk. According to S&P Global, OpenAI accounts for roughly half of Oracle's $638 billion in contractual obligations, which raises alarms about Oracle's financial stability. This decision highlights the increasing influence of AI partnerships on corporate creditworthiness. The downgrade reflects a growing trend in which AI entities are seen as pivotal to the financial health of established tech companies. Practitioners should note that this shift may affect Oracle's ability to secure favorable financing terms as it navigates its obligations linked to OpenAI.
