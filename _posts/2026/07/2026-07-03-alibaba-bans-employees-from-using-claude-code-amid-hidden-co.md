Alibaba has implemented a ban on its employees using Claude Code after the discovery of hidden code that could identify Chinese users. This decision follows concerns raised by ByteDance and Ant Financial, both of which are attempting to access Claude Code through overseas subsidiaries and VPNs to circumvent restrictions. The situation highlights the complexities of AI access and compliance in China, where companies are navigating both internal bans and external access challenges. This follows a trend of increasing scrutiny over AI tools in the region, as companies seek to leverage advanced technologies while adhering to local regulations.
