# Neill Blomkamp's 'Nightborne' is First Short Film Generated with AI Video Model

Neill Blomkamp has released 'Nightborne', a 13-minute sci-fi horror short film generated entirely using the Seedance 2.0 video model. This marks a significant milestone as it is the first film of its kind produced by Blomkamp's AI film studio, Barley Studios. The use of Seedance 2.0 showcases advancements in AI video generation capabilities, potentially influencing future projects in the film industry. For practitioners, this development highlights the increasing feasibility of creating full-length narratives using AI-generated content, as seen in Blomkamp's innovative approach.
