OpenAI Academy is launching hands-on AI Skills Jams specifically for K–12 educators. This initiative, in collaboration with the Walton Family Foundation, aims to equip educators with practical AI skills necessary for integrating technology into their teaching practices. The program targets educators directly, addressing a growing need for AI literacy in the classroom. This follows OpenAI's ongoing efforts to enhance educational resources in AI, as seen in their previous initiatives aimed at broader educational engagement. For practitioners, this development provides a structured opportunity to enhance their AI competencies, which can directly influence curriculum design and student engagement strategies. More details can be found on the [OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/k-12-educators-practical-skills).
