OpenAI, DeepSeek, and Meta are part of a mass exodus from Nvidia, seeking custom chip solutions for their AI workloads. This shift highlights a growing trend among leading AI organizations to develop proprietary hardware tailored to their specific needs. The move may impact Nvidia's market share in the AI hardware sector, which has been dominated by its GPUs. As these companies pivot to custom solutions, practitioners may need to adapt their development strategies to accommodate new hardware architectures.
