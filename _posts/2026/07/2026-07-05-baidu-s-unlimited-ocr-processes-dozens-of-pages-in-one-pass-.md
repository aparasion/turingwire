Baidu's Unlimited OCR can process dozens of document pages in a single pass, significantly improving upon previous systems that were limited to ten pages. This advancement is attributed to a modified attention mechanism that maintains flat memory usage regardless of the number of pages processed. Currently, Unlimited OCR holds the top position on the most important OCR benchmark, indicating its leading performance in the field. This follows Baidu's recent developments in AI tools, reinforcing their commitment to enhancing document processing capabilities. Practitioners can leverage this technology for more efficient document analysis and processing workflows.
