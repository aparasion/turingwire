NVIDIA's Nemotron 3 Ultra model claims to achieve the highest accuracy among open models while completing tasks at a throughput running 10x faster than previous iterations. This performance is reported to be at a lower cost compared to leading closed models. The integration with LangChain enhances its capabilities as an AI agent orchestration platform, optimizing task management and execution. LangChain attributes the accuracy claim to its orchestration technology, which is designed to maximize the efficiency of AI models. This development follows NVIDIA's recent advancements in AI infrastructure and software solutions, reinforcing their commitment to open models in AI research. Practitioners can expect improved performance metrics and cost efficiency when deploying the Nemotron 3 Ultra in their applications, as noted by [NVIDIA Blog](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/nemotron-langchain-agents-open-stack/).
