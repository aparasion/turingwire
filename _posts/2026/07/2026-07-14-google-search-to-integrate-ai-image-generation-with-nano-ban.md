Google will begin rolling out AI image generation in Search using the Nano Banana 2 Lite model in the coming weeks. This feature will automatically create images when the search engine cannot find relevant results. This development enhances the user experience by providing visual content directly in response to queries that yield no existing images. As a result, developers and product managers can leverage this capability to enrich applications that rely on Google Search for content retrieval. This follows Google's ongoing efforts to integrate advanced AI functionalities into its services, as seen in previous updates to their AI Overviews. For more details, see [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/google-search-now-generates-ai-images-when-it-cant-find-what-youre-looking-for-on-the-web/).
