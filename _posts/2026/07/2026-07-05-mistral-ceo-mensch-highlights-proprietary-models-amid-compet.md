Mistral's CEO Arthur Mensch stated that the company cannot compete with frontier models from OpenAI and Anthropic on performance. Mensch emphasized Mistral's strategic focus on EU sovereignty as a competitive edge in the AI market. He expressed concerns about AI labs storing increasing amounts of customer data, which in some cases has been used to target their own customers as competitors. This positioning may influence how organizations approach partnerships with AI providers, particularly in the EU context. Mistral's strategy follows its recent efforts to secure funding and establish a robust data foundation, as noted in previous coverage by [The Decoder](https://the-decoder.com/mistral-ceo-mensch-says-proprietary-ai-models-give-labs-a-front-row-seat-to-your-business-processes/).
