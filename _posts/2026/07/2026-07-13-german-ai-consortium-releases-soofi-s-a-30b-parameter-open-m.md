Soofi S, an open language model with 31.6 billion parameters, has been released by a German research consortium. The model is specifically weighted toward the German language and claims to outperform all fully open competitors on benchmarks in both German and English, according to the consortium. Deutsche Telekom provides the cloud infrastructure for this initiative. This release adds to the growing landscape of open models, following the recent advancements in AI from other major players like OpenAI and Google DeepMind. Developers can leverage Soofi S for applications requiring high performance in both languages, enhancing multilingual capabilities in AI solutions.
