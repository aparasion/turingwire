OpenAI has launched **OpenAI Presence**, a proven enterprise AI agent platform designed to help organizations deploy trusted voice and chat agents for both customer and internal workflows. This platform aims to streamline communication processes within enterprises, enhancing operational efficiency. OpenAI emphasizes the platform's reliability in managing interactions, which could significantly impact how businesses implement AI in their customer service and internal operations. The introduction of Presence follows OpenAI's ongoing efforts to enhance AI deployment strategies, as seen in their recent initiatives on AI safety and ROI measurement in AI deployments, reported earlier this month. For more details, visit the [OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-presence).
