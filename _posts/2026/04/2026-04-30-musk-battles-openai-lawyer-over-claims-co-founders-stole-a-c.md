The lawyer highlighted inconsistencies in Musk's recollections of the organization’s early days, using Musk's own emails to counter his narrative. This exchange not only cast doubt on Musk's motivations but also emphasized the complexities surrounding the founding principles of OpenAI, which was established with a mission to prioritize safety and ethical considerations in AI development.
