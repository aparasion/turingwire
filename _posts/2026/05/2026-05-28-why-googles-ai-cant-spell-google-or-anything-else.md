The timing is critical, as the company faces increasing competition from rivals like OpenAI and Microsoft, who are making significant strides in the AI space.

The implications are significant: if Google cannot deliver on fundamental capabilities, it risks losing ground to competitors who are rapidly advancing their own AI technologies.
