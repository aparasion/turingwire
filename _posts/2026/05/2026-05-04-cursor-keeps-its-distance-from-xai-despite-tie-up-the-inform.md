Despite the partnership, Cursor is keen on keeping its distance from xAI's more aggressive tactics and public persona. This decision underscores Cursor's commitment to a more measured and user-centric approach in the AI space, appealing to users who may be wary of the rapid and sometimes reckless advancements associated with xAI.
