This launch is particularly timely as businesses and consumers increasingly seek more human-like interactions with technology, a trend that has gained momentum in recent years.

The Sesame app aims to redefine user experience by enabling more natural and fluid conversations with AI agents, moving away from the rigid, scripted responses typical of traditional chatbots. This approach is designed to enhance user engagement and satisfaction, potentially making AI interactions feel more intuitive and less transactional. The app's capabilities are built on advanced natural language processing techniques, allowing it to understand context and respond in a manner that mimics human conversation. While specific user metrics or performance benchmarks have not been disclosed, the founders emphasize the app's potential to transform customer service, personal assistance, and other sectors reliant on conversational interfaces.
