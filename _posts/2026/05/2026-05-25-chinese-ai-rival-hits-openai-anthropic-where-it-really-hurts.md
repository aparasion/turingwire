Users may benefit from increased competition, leading to more innovative features and lower costs. However, it also raises concerns about the implications of a rapidly evolving AI landscape, particularly regarding data privacy and ethical considerations.

As the competitive landscape continues to evolve, it will be crucial to monitor how established companies respond to this new threat and whether they can maintain their leadership positions in the face of aggressive pricing and innovation from rivals.
