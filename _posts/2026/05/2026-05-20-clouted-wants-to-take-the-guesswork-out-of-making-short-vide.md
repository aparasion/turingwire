Clouted, a startup focused on optimizing short video content for virality, has successfully secured a $7 million seed funding round led by Slow Ventures. This funding comes at a pivotal moment as the demand for engaging short-form video content continues to surge across social media platforms, making it essential for creators and brands to stand out in a crowded market.

The company aims to eliminate the uncertainty surrounding what makes videos go viral by leveraging data-driven insights and AI algorithms. Clouted’s platform analyzes trends, audience preferences, and engagement metrics to provide creators with actionable recommendations on video content, style, and timing. This approach not only streamlines the content creation process but also enhances the likelihood of reaching wider audiences. With the short video market expected to grow significantly, Clouted positions itself as a critical tool for content creators, brands, and marketers looking to maximize their online presence.
