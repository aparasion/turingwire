This collaboration aims to streamline the governance processes for organizations leveraging AI, addressing a critical need as businesses increasingly integrate AI into their operations.

The partnership will focus on creating solutions that simplify the complexities of AI governance, ensuring compliance with regulations and ethical standards. Persistent Systems, known for its software development and technology services, will leverage Kong's API management capabilities to facilitate better control and monitoring of AI systems. This initiative comes at a time when regulatory scrutiny over AI applications is intensifying, prompting companies to prioritize governance frameworks that can mitigate risks associated with AI deployment. The collaboration is expected to provide enterprises with tools that not only enhance compliance but also improve operational efficiency.
