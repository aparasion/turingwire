This development is particularly noteworthy as it enhances the functionality of X, the platform formerly known as Twitter, which is increasingly competing for user engagement and content visibility. The timing is crucial, as social media platforms are continuously evolving to retain users and attract advertisers amid growing competition.

The update allows users to leverage the Phoenix ranking engine to better curate and prioritize content. Notably, the update emphasizes the impact of user interactions, with one reply now holding more weight than 150 likes in determining content visibility.
