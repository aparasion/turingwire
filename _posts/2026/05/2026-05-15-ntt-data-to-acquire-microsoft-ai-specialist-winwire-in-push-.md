NTT DATA aims to enhance its offerings in data analytics, machine learning, and AI-driven automation, which are increasingly critical for enterprises seeking to improve efficiency and drive innovation. The financial details of the deal have not been disclosed, but it is expected to bolster NTT DATA's competitive edge in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
