The company plans to invest significantly in building AI data centers across key European locations, aiming to enhance the region's capabilities in machine learning and data processing. This initiative is expected to cater to a diverse range of industries, from finance to healthcare, which are eager to adopt AI solutions. Phoenix Group's decision is also a response to the competitive landscape, as tech giants like Google and Microsoft have already established a strong presence in Europe.
