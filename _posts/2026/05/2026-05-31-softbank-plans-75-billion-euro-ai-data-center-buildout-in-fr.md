This initiative represents the company's largest AI infrastructure project in Europe to date and aims to establish facilities with a total capacity of 5 gigawatts by 2031. The announcement underscores SoftBank's aggressive push into the AI sector, but it also raises questions about the feasibility and timeline of such ambitious projects.

The planned investment includes the construction of facilities worth 45 billion euros at three sites in northern France. This move is part of a broader strategy by SoftBank to expand its global footprint in AI and data processing capabilities, which are increasingly critical as demand for AI services surges.
