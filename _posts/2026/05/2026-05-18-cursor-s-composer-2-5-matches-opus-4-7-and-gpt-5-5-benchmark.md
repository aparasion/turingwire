Cursor has launched Composer 2.5, an advanced AI coding model that not only matches the performance of established benchmarks like Opus 4.7 and GPT-5.5 but does so at a significantly lower cost. This release comes at a crucial time as companies seek more affordable yet effective AI solutions to enhance their development processes.

The new Composer 2.5 is built on the Kimi K2.5 architecture and has been trained on 25 times more synthetic tasks than its predecessor, indicating a substantial leap in its training methodology. This increase in training data is expected to improve the model's accuracy and versatility in coding tasks, making it a compelling option for developers looking to streamline their workflows. By achieving benchmark parity with more expensive models, Cursor positions itself as a cost-effective alternative in a competitive market, potentially reshaping pricing strategies across the AI coding landscape.
