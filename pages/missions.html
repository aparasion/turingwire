Missions

Define what you want to keep tracking — companies, keywords, impact level — and Turing Wire treats each mission as a standing goal: the index reruns on every visit, surfaces only items new since you last checked, and lets you export the result as JSON or a pinnable RSS-style URL.

How matching works

  • An item matches if any company filter matches AND any keyword filter matches (or filters are empty).
  • Impact is hierarchical: major+ includes major and critical.
  • "Since you last checked" uses the timestamp of your previous visit to this page; new items are highlighted.
  • The 90-second brief format renders the top 5 matches as a contiguous read.
